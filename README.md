# PROJECT: ENIGMA 35

> **The truth is not held by one, but divided among all.**

`PROJECT: ENIGMA 35` is a global, collaborative Alternate Reality Game (ARG) and interactive web quest. It is designed as a psychological and intellectual experiment where progress is impossible for a single isolated individual. To unlock the system, humanity must act as a collective mind.

---

## 🌍 The Lore & Concept

The countdown has begun. A locked system stands before the world, and the master key is missing. However, the key wasn't destroyed—it was shattered into **28 unique fragments** and scattered across the digital space.

When a player visits the terminal, they are automatically assigned one random puzzle piece (e.g., `T-1`, `H-2`, etc.). No single player can see the other 27 pieces on their own. The global counter on the dashboard will not budge unless players actively talk to each other, share what they found, and manually input fragments discovered by others. 

Only by combining forces, breaking language barriers, and building a community can players piece together the global key and bypass the security layers.

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. **Discover Your Fragment:** Look at your dashboard to find your unique piece of the puzzle.
2. **Spread the Word:** Use the "Share Project" feature to generate your unique cipher text and invite others.
3. **Collaborate:** Search communities, social media, or group chats for other players' fragments. 
4. **Feed the Matrix:** Manually input found fragments into your terminal to track global progress.
5. **Decipher the Key:** Once enough pieces are found, assemble the final Global Key, enter it, and see what lies on the other side.
